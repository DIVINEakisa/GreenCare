How It Works

A simple 5-step process to improve waste management

1

Waste Separation at Source

Users separate waste into biodegradable and non-biodegradable categories right at home, school, or market.

2

Smart Bin Detection

Our smart bin system helps detect incorrect waste input and provides real-time feedback to ensure proper separation.

3

Collection & Transport

Well-separated waste is collected from households, schools, and markets through an organized collection schedule.

4

Processing & Transformation

Biodegradable waste is processed into nutrient-rich fertilizer while non-biodegradable waste goes for proper recycling.

5

Value Creation

Transformed resources return to communities as affordable fertilizer and recycled products, closing the circular economy loop.
